My Design Process

Creating User Personas

Personas represent our most common target audience. It helps me understand user needs and get standardized solutions. In this process I get a chance to create an imaginary friend with a name, photo, and brief description.


Research

Market-, user- and design- research are the 3 main pillars for the foundation of a good project. I make sure that all these three key parameters are thoroughly inspected in my process.


Sitemap/ User-flow

After understanding the users need and market trends my third step is site mapping and determining user flow.


Wireframing and UX designing

This step is the actual design step which gets me all excited and the project starts getting into its final stage.
